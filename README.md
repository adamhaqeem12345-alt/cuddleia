# Cuddleia — Cozy Digital Goods

Cuddleia is a professional digital storefront designed to bring warmth, beauty, and serenity to digital spaces. Built with a focus on ethical commerce and aesthetic design, the platform offers thoughtfully crafted booklets and wallpapers that inspire productivity and peace.

## 🌟 The Vision
Cuddleia represents the intersection of technology and sincerity. Our mission is to transform the digital experience into a place of comfort and reflection, providing high-quality digital tools for the modern user.

## ✨ Key Features
- **The Barakah Business Blueprint:** A comprehensive 5-volume digital series designed to help entrepreneurs build ethical, faith-rooted businesses.
- **Aesthetic Digital Goods:** High-resolution, custom-designed iPad and tablet wallpapers featuring minimalist art and curated color palettes.
- **Seamless E-commerce:** A fully integrated shopping experience with real-time currency conversion (USD to MYR) and secure global checkout.
- **Ethical AI Integration:** Content is intelligently generated using AI and then manually refined and designed to ensure a professional, human touch.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14 (App Router), React 18, TypeScript.
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS and ShadCN UI components.
- **AI Integration:** Firebase Genkit utilizing Gemini models for structured content generation.
- **Payments:** Integrated PayPal SDK for secure international transactions and multi-method payment support.
- **Infrastructure:** Hosted with Firebase, utilizing Server Actions for dynamic data handling and currency conversion.

## ⚙️ Business Automation & Logic
- **Real-time Notifications:** Integrated with the **Telegram Bot API** to provide instant owner notifications for sales and inquiries.
- **Data Persistence:** Automated sales logging to **Google Sheets** via the Google Sheets API for streamlined business tracking.
- **Automated Fulfillment:** Custom **Nodemailer** integration with Zoho Mail to deliver digital products to customers instantly upon successful payment.
- **Dynamic Pricing:** Real-time exchange rate fetching to provide localized currency estimates, improving the user experience for regional markets.
